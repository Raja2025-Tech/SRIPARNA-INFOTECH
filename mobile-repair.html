Mobile Repair FAQs

How long does a screen replacement take?

For common models where parts are in stock, screen replacements can often be completed the same day. Rare models may take 1-2 days to source parts.

Do you repair both Android and iPhones?

Is my data safe during repair?

We respect your privacy. While we never access personal files, we always recommend backing up your data before any major hardware repair if the phone is functional.
