Behind the Screens

The story of grit, family, and building a dream from the ground up.

Every business has a beginning, but very few start with a silver spoon. The journey of Sriparna Infotech was forged in the fire of real-life struggles, countless sleepless nights, and the sheer refusal to give up.

My name is Subhasish Das, and I am the Proprietor of this company. If you are reading this, I want to be entirely honest with you: this journey has not been easy. Life has thrown endless challenges my way. I have faced moments where everything seemed to break down, where the pressure of building a secure future felt impossible to carry. Even today, as the business stands in its growing stage, there are days when I have to break myself down just to rebuild stronger the next morning.

But that is exactly why I understand the value of hard work, and more importantly, the value of your trust. When you bring your broken devices or security needs to us, you aren't just getting a technical fix. You are getting the dedication of someone who knows what it takes to restore things to their full potential.

The Roots

Sriparna Infotech is proudly named after my mother, Sriparna Das. This company carries her name because it is built on the values she taught me: uncompromising integrity, unwavering strength, and the deepest level of care.

The Future

Just as we honor the past, we build for tomorrow. Our sister venture, Shreya Trading, is named after my beloved daughter, Shreya Das. She is the reason I push through every obstacle, ensuring that every step forward is a step toward a brighter legacy.

"In technology and in life, things will inevitably break down. True success isn't about avoiding the crash—it's about having the heart, the grit, and the knowledge to rebuild it stronger than before."

— Subhasish Das

We are still growing, still learning, and still fighting every day to achieve success. But no matter how big we get, we will always remember where we came from. Thank you for being a part of our journey, and for trusting our family to serve yours.
