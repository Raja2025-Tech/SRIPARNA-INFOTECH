The Story Behind the Journey

This is not a story written after success. This is a story being written on the way to success.

My name is Subhasish Das. I didn't begin my entrepreneurial journey with unlimited capital, powerful connections, a massive team, or a flawless roadmap. My path has been paved with struggle, uncertainty, heavy responsibilities, and moments where simply moving forward felt incredibly difficult.

But stopping was never an option.

Every difficult stage taught me something invaluable. Every setback forced me to rebuild myself stronger. Every small, hard-earned achievement became another foundation stone. SRIPARNA INFOTECH is being built step by step—with persistence, honest hard work, and the unwavering determination to create something meaningful in the technology sector.

Building forward, step by step.
