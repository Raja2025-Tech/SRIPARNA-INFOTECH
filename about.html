A Journey of Grit and Generations

"Every time life broke me down, it was just handing me the bricks to build my legacy."

Welcome to our story.

I am Subhasish Das, the proprietor, and this business is not a tale of overnight success or easy victories. It is a story of relentless struggle, survival, and the profound power of family.

In 2016, this dream took its very first breath as Sriparna Infotech. It started out unregistered, raw, and fueled entirely by passion. I named it in honor of my mother, Sriparna Das. She represents the foundation of my life, the deep roots that kept me grounded when the hardest storms hit.

And those storms have been relentless. My path has been paved with immense challenges, and even today, we are still in a growing stage. Every single step forward has often felt like it was breaking me apart, demanding that I rebuild a new, stronger version of myself just to keep reaching for success. But true growth requires that kind of breaking.

Roots are meant to grow into branches. As the years passed and the struggles forged a sharper, more resilient vision, our legacy evolved. In 2025, we took a massive step forward and officially registered under the MSME Udyam as Shreya Trading. This new chapter is named after my daughter, Shreya Das—she is the future, the light, and the ultimate reason I refuse to quit.

Today, Sriparna Infotech operates proudly as a specialized unit of Shreya Trading. From the foundation laid by my mother to the future inspired by my daughter, this business is bookended by the two most important generations of my life.

We are still growing, still fighting, and still building. Thank you for supporting our journey and becoming a part of our continuing story.
