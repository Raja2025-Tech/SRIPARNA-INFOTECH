About Sriparna Infotech

Built Through Struggle. Named Through Love. Growing With Purpose.

"Every setback shaped us. Every struggle strengthened us. Every step forward is building the future we once only dreamed about."

Sriparna Infotech is more than a business name. It is a story that began with ambition, continued through struggle, and is still being written today.

Where It All Began — 2016

In 2016, Subhasish Das started Sriparna Infotech as an unregistered small venture with limited resources but a strong desire to build something of his own.

The name "Sriparna" was chosen in honour of his mother, Sriparna Das — a name that represents the roots, support and inspiration behind the journey.

There was no big office. No large team. No major investment. Only a willingness to work, learn, adapt and keep moving forward.

The journey was never a straight road. There were difficult days, financial challenges, uncertainty, mistakes and moments when starting again seemed harder than giving up. But the dream survived.

From Sriparna Infotech to Shreya Trading

Years of experience and persistence eventually led to an important milestone. In 2025, the business journey entered a new chapter with the registration of Shreya Trading under the MSME Udyam Registration framework.

The name "Shreya" comes from the next generation — Shreya Das, daughter of Proprietor Subhasish Das.

Today, Sriparna Infotech operates as a unit of Shreya Trading.

Two names. Two generations. One journey.

Sriparna represents the roots from which the dream began.
Shreya represents the future for which the dream continues.

And between them stands a journey built by Subhasish Das — still learning, still working and still growing.

Our Approach

Based in the heart of Barasat, Sriparna Infotech was established to provide the community with a reliable, straightforward IT and electronics repair service. We realized that when a laptop dies or a printer jams, people don't want technical jargon—they want a clear diagnosis, a fair price, and a working device.

No Fix, No Fee Policy

If we cannot repair your device or diagnose the issue, you don't pay for the service attempt. It's our guarantee of honest workmanship.

We Are Still Growing

We do not want to tell visitors a perfect success story, because our story is not about overnight success. We are still in our growing stage.

Every challenge has taught us something. Every failure has forced us to improve. At different stages of life, we have had to break old limitations, rebuild ourselves and find another way forward.

There is still a long road ahead. But that is exactly what makes this journey meaningful.

We believe a business does not become trustworthy simply because it looks big. Trust is built by taking responsibility, respecting customers, delivering honest work and standing behind the services we provide. That is the foundation on which we want to build our future.

Our Purpose

At Sriparna Infotech, a unit of Shreya Trading, our purpose is simple: to make useful technology and digital services more accessible, dependable and practical for the people and businesses we serve in Barasat and the surrounding areas.

We believe every customer matters. Every project carries responsibility. Every opportunity deserves our best effort.

We may still be growing, but our commitment is clear — to keep learning, keep improving and build something that our family, our customers and the next generation can be proud of.
