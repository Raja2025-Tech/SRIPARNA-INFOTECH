Our Story

"Every step forward required breaking my old self to build a successful future."

The Genesis: A Mother's Blessing

Every great institution is built on a foundation of unyielding support. For us, that foundation was family. Back in 2016, a dream took root in a small, unregistered setup. It wasn't backed by massive capital or corporate investors; it was fueled entirely by passion, grit, and the blessings of a mother.

That is why the name Sriparna Infotech was born—a direct tribute to Sriparna Das. It served as a daily reminder that no matter how difficult the technology industry became, the core values of care, dedication, and integrity would always remain at the heart of our service.

We didn't just build a business.
We forged it from the pieces of a thousand setbacks.

Success isn't handed out; it is painstakingly assembled through endless nights, relentless struggles, and the courage to start over when things fall apart.

The Next Generation: Formalizing the Dream

The journey from 2016 was never a straight line. There were times when the weight of the struggle felt insurmountable. But the vision was larger than just one person. It was about creating a legacy.

In 2025, that relentless drive culminated in a proud milestone. We formally registered under the MSME Udyam Certificate as Shreya Trading—named with immense love and pride after our daughter, Shreya Das. Today, Sriparna Infotech operates proudly as a dedicated unit of Shreya Trading, bridging the wisdom of the past generation with the bright promise of the next.

From the Founder's Desk

If you are reading this, I want to personally thank you for visiting our digital home.

People often see a functioning business and assume the path was easy. The truth is, there have been too many struggles in my life to count. Even today, we are in a growing stage. I have learned that true growth requires transformation—sometimes, you have to let your old self break so that a stronger, more successful version of yourself can be built.

When you bring your computer, mobile, or security needs to Sriparna Infotech, you aren't just a ticket number. You are supporting a family's dream. Because I know the value of hard work, I know the exact value of your time and your hard-earned devices. We will treat them, and you, with the utmost respect.

Subhasish Das
