Our Story

Behind every repaired motherboard and installed camera is a story of grit, family, and the refusal to give up.

Every successful business has a foundation, but ours wasn't built on venture capital or a flawless master plan. It was built on struggle, late nights, and the profound love of a family. I am Subhasish Das, the founder of Sriparna Infotech, and this is our journey.

In 2016, this company was just a dream—unregistered, unfunded, but relentlessly driven. I named it Sriparna Infotech after my mother, Sriparna Das. She has always been the ultimate symbol of unconditional support and endurance in my life. I wanted a name that carried her blessing, a name that reminded me every day to keep pushing forward, no matter how hard the road became.

"Every step that broke me down, was just assembling the foundation of my success."

— Subhasish Das, Proprietor

And the road was incredibly hard. The path of an entrepreneur is never a straight line. There were times when the financial hurdles seemed insurmountable, when the technical challenges kept me awake until dawn, and when the weight of failure felt too heavy to carry. My life has been filled with intense struggles, and to be completely honest, we are still in our growing stage. But I realized early on that resilience is like a muscle—it has to be torn to grow stronger.

As the years passed and the struggles began to bear fruit, a new generation brought new inspiration. In 2025, we took a massive leap forward and officially registered the business under the MSME Udyam Certificate. But this time, it was named after the future: my daughter, Shreya Das.

Today, Sriparna Infotech operates proudly as a dedicated unit of Shreya Trading. It represents the perfect bridge between honoring my roots and building a legacy for the next generation.

Sriparna Infotech — A Unit of Shreya Trading (MSME Registered)

When you bring your computer, laptop, or security needs to us in Barasat, you aren't just a customer to a faceless corporation. You are putting your trust in a business that knows the true value of hard work. We treat your devices with the exact same dedication, care, and resilience that it took to build this company.

Thank you for being part of our story. We are still writing it, one repair at a time.
